About

Techie Bots is the student body of NGP Patna-13.

The elected representatives of the students strive to foster growth and leadership among the students by coordinating various events helping to extract the maximum of ones talent. To promote the co-curricular activities and interests, the Techie Bots has many clubs and groups dedicated to the interests of the students.
